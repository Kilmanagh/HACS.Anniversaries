📋 Available Categories

  • birthday - 🎂 Birthday anniversaries
  • anniversary - 💍 Wedding/relationship anniversaries
  • memorial - 🌸 Memorial and remembrance dates
  • holiday - 🎉 Holiday and seasonal celebrations
  • work - 💼 Work and employment anniversaries
  • achievement - 🏆 Achievements and accomplishments
  • event - 📅 General events and milestones
  • other - 📋 Miscellaneous anniversaries

🔧 Key Enhancements

  • ✅ Category filtering: Display only specific anniversary types
  • ✅ Smart defaults: Auto-generated titles, icons, and attributes
  • ✅ Long date format: Local language date formatting
  • ✅ Category-aware icons: Proper emoji selection based on category
  • ✅ Backward compatibility: Existing cards continue working
